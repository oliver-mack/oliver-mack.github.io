Who we are

We are a company that develops new ways to drink by combining crowd-sourced data with beautiful design. Our main product is a device that you can use wherever you are and implements new creative techniques. Our target audience is seniors. We want to convey a sense of security, while at the same time being modern.

Our mission

Our mission is to bring comfort and confidence to every sip, ensuring that seniors feel both cared for and empowered. By blending intuitive technology with elegant design, we create products that are easy to use, safe, and adaptable to individual needs. Every detail of our device is crafted to provide reassurance while maintaining a fresh, modern aesthetic because we believe that staying in the know should be both effortless and enjoyable, no matter where life takes you.
