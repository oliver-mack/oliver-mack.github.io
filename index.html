    About my work

    I am a student journalist, filmmaker, photographer and writer. I currently serve as the Deputy Managing Editor of The Diamondback, UMD's independent student newspaper. I began at The Diamondback as a Staff Writer in fall of 2024. I wrote more than 60 stories in my time on the news desk, covering campus events, protests, and state and federal politics. I have also written a slew of stories with more of a local focus for College Park Here and Now. I serve as the business reporter for WMUC's Weekly Roundup and I interned with The Baltimore Banner during the spring of 2024.
